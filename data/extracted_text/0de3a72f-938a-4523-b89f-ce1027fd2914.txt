 HR Leave and Attendance Policy
Purpose: This file is part of a sample multi-document project for testing API-key protected RAG upload, indexing,
semantic search, chat answers, and source citations.
Overview
This policy explains working hours, attendance expectations, leave categories, approval rules, and
documentation requirements for employees.
Employees are expected to record attendance accurately every working day. Missing attendance
records must be regularized within three working days.
Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM from Monday to Friday. A flexible grace period of 15
minutes is allowed up to three times in a month.
Repeated late login beyond the grace limit may be marked as half-day leave after manager review.
Leave Types
Casual leave can be used for short personal needs and should be requested at least one day in
advance whenever possible.
Sick leave can be taken for health reasons. If sick leave exceeds two consecutive working days, a
medical certificate may be requested.
Earned leave must be planned and approved at least seven days before the start date unless an
exception is approved by HR.
Leave Type
Summary
Casual Leave
Short personal needs; advance notice preferred.
Sick Leave
Health reasons; certificate may be needed after two days.
Earned Leave
Planned leave; seven-day advance approval recommended.
Approval Rules
All leave requests must be submitted through the official HR portal or approved communication
channel.
Managers should approve or reject leave requests within two working days. HR will review policy
exceptions.
Attendance Regularization
Employees may regularize missed punches, incorrect login times, or approved work-from-home
attendance within three working days.
Regularization requests must include a clear reason and manager confirmation.
