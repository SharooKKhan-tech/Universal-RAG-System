 Product Warranty and Returns Guide
Purpose: This file is part of a sample multi-document project for testing API-key protected RAG upload, indexing,
semantic search, chat answers, and source citations.
Overview
This guide explains product warranty coverage, return eligibility, replacement conditions, refund
timelines, and customer support process.
Warranty and returns are handled according to product category, purchase proof, issue type, and
usage condition.
Warranty Coverage
Standard electronic products include a one-year limited warranty from the date of purchase.
Accessories such as cables, chargers, and adapters include a six-month limited warranty unless
otherwise mentioned on the invoice.
Warranty covers manufacturing defects, power failure caused by internal faults, and non-physical
component malfunction.
Product Category
Warranty Period
Electronics
One year limited warranty
Accessories
Six months limited warranty
Replacement Unit
Remaining original warranty or ninety days, whichever is longer
Return Eligibility
Customers may request a return within seven calendar days of delivery if the product is unused,
undamaged, and includes original packaging.
Opened products may be eligible for replacement if a verified manufacturing defect is found during
inspection.
Refund Timeline
Approved refunds are initiated within five to seven business days after the returned product passes
inspection.
Refunds are sent to the original payment method. Bank processing time may vary by payment
provider.
Warranty Exclusions
Warranty does not cover physical damage, water damage, unauthorized repair, misuse, normal wear
and tear, or missing serial numbers.
Products without valid purchase proof may be rejected for warranty service.
