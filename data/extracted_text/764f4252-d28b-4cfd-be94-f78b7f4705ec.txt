 Employee Travel and Expense Policy
Purpose: This file is part of a sample multi-document project for testing API-key protected RAG upload, indexing,
semantic search, chat answers, and source citations.
Overview
This policy describes travel approvals, booking rules, reimbursement requirements, meal limits, and
expense claim timelines.
Employees must use reasonable judgment and choose cost-effective travel options for business trips.
Travel Approval
Domestic travel must be approved by the reporting manager before booking.
International travel must be approved by the department head and finance team before tickets are
purchased.
Emergency travel may be approved through email confirmation and regularized after the trip.
Booking Rules
Economy class must be used for domestic flights. Premium travel requires written department-head
approval.
Hotel bookings should follow the approved city-wise budget limits unless the client or event location
requires an exception.
Reimbursement
Expense claims must be submitted within ten working days after travel completion.
Original bills or digital receipts are required for hotel, flight, taxi, and meal expenses.
Claims without receipts may be rejected unless finance approves a documented exception.

Meal and Local Transport Limits
Meal reimbursement is allowed for business travel days only and must follow the company expense
limits.
Local transport should use standard taxi, rideshare, bus, metro, or approved rental services.
