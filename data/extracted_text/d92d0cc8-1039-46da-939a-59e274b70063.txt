1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how Medi-Scan ("Medi-Scan", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, shares, and
protects information when you use our mobile application, website, AI assistant, document scanning tools,
medication reminder system, emergency alert features, and related services collectively referred to as the
"Services".
Medi-Scan is an AI-powered smart health companion designed to help users digitize prescriptions, understand
blood test reports, manage medicine reminders, maintain health records, calculate BMI, track medication
adherence, receive diet suggestions, and ask health-related questions through MediBot.
2. Medical Disclaimer
Medi-Scan provides informational and organizational support only. It does not provide medical diagnosis,
treatment, prescription decisions, emergency care, or professional medical advice.
AI-generated report explanations, prescription summaries, safety checks, dietary recommendations, and MediBot
responses may be incomplete or inaccurate. You must consult a qualified doctor, pharmacist, or healthcare
provider before making medical decisions, changing medication, stopping medication, or relying on health
suggestions.
In a medical emergency, contact local emergency services immediately.
3. Definitions and Scope
Personal Data means information that identifies or can reasonably identify an individual, such as name, email
address, phone number, account details, device information, or location information.
Health Data means information related to physical or medical condition, prescriptions, medicines, dosage, lab
reports, diagnostic values, BMI, weight, symptoms, medicine history, allergies, health profile, or wellness data.
Medical Documents means prescriptions, blood test reports, lab report PDFs, images, doctor notes, medical
slips, and other health-related files uploaded or scanned through Medi-Scan.
This Policy applies to Medi-Scan users who access or use our Services. It does not apply to third-party websites,
hospitals, labs, doctors, pharmacies, payment providers, or external services that have their own privacy policies.

5. Location Usage
Medi-Scan may collect and use your location only when location-based features are enabled and permission is
granted by you. The primary location feature is emergency missed-dose alerting for critical medications.
If a critical dose is missed and emergency alerts are enabled, Medi-Scan may include your last known GPS
location or approximate location in the alert sent to your selected emergency contact.
Medi-Scan does not use location for advertising. You can disable location permission through device settings, but
emergency missed-dose alerts may not include location after permission is disabled.
6. Payments
If Medi-Scan offers paid subscriptions, premium features, consultations, reports, or in-app purchases, payment
processing may be handled by third-party payment processors, app stores, or billing providers.
Medi-Scan does not store full credit card numbers, debit card numbers, CVV, UPI PINs, banking passwords, or
complete payment credentials on our own servers. Payment providers may collect billing name, payment method
details, transaction amount, invoice information, tax information, and payment status according to their own privacy
policies.
We may store limited payment-related records such as subscription status, transaction ID, plan type, purchase
date, renewal date, refund status, and billing support communications to manage accounts, comply with legal
obligations, and provide customer support.

8. Data Sharing
We do not sell personal health information, prescriptions, medical reports, medication history, emergency contact
information, or location information. We share information only as needed to provide Medi-Scan services, protect
users, comply with law, or complete business operations.
8.1 Service Providers
We may share limited information with trusted providers that help us operate the app, including cloud hosting,
databases, OTP delivery, push notifications, AI/OCR processing, email delivery, analytics, crash reporting,
customer support, security monitoring, and payment processing.
8.2 Emergency Contacts
If you enable emergency alerts, we may share missed critical dose information and last known location with your
selected emergency contact.
8.3 Healthcare Providers, Caregivers, or Family Members
We share health records with doctors, caregivers, family members, or other people only when you choose to share
them or use a feature that sends information to them.
8.4 Legal, Safety, and Business Transfers
We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, regulatory request, safety concern, fraud prevention
need, security incident, enforcement of our terms, merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets.

10. Data Storage and Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user information, including secure
transmission, secure storage, OTP authentication, access controls, encryption where appropriate, monitoring, and
limited access to sensitive data.
Because no electronic system is completely secure, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible
for keeping your device, phone number, email account, and app access secure.
11. Data Retention
We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide Medi-Scan services, comply with law, resolve
disputes, prevent fraud or misuse, maintain backups, and improve security.
Medical records are retained until you delete them or close your account, unless longer retention is required or
permitted by law. Reminder history, adherence logs, emergency contact details, and location alerts are retained
only as needed for the relevant features. Technical logs may be retained for a limited period for security and
debugging.
12. Account Deletion
You may request deletion of your Medi-Scan account through the in-app account deletion option, if available, or by
contacting us at [Insert Privacy / Support Email].
After a verified deletion request, we will delete or anonymize personal data, health data, uploaded documents,
medication history, reminder data, emergency contact details, and account information unless retention is required
for legal, security, fraud prevention, backup, dispute resolution, tax, accounting, or legitimate business purposes
permitted by law.
Some deleted information may remain in encrypted backups for a limited period before permanent removal.
13. Age Restriction and Children's Privacy
Medi-Scan is intended for users who are at least 18 years old. Users under 18 may use the Services only with the
involvement and consent of a parent or legal guardian, where permitted by law.
We do not knowingly collect personal or health information from children without appropriate consent. If we learn
that we collected such information without required consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.
Parents or guardians may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of a child's data.

15. AI Processing and Health Insights
Medi-Scan uses AI technologies such as OCR, handwriting recognition, text extraction, natural language
processing, retrieval-based context, and AI-assisted summarization to support prescription scanning, lab report
explanation, MediBot responses, diet suggestions, duplicate medicine checks, and safety alerts.
Unless we separately ask for your permission, we do not use identifiable prescriptions, medical reports, health
records, or personal health data to train public AI models. We may use anonymized or aggregated data to improve
reliability, safety, and performance where permitted by law.
AI results should be reviewed carefully. Users must verify extracted medicines, dosages, timings, report values,
and emergency contact settings before relying on them.
16. User Rights and Choices
Depending on applicable law and your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, restrict,
object to, or withdraw consent for certain processing of your personal data.
●
Access and review personal data and health records stored in your account.
●
Correct inaccurate account, medicine, report, or emergency contact information.
●
Delete uploaded records, prescriptions, reports, reminders, or your entire account.
●
Withdraw consent for optional features such as notifications, location sharing, emergency alerts, and AI
personalization.
●
Disable permissions from device settings, including camera, files, notifications, location, and contacts.
●
Contact support for privacy requests, complaints, or account assistance.
